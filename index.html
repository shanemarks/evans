Welcome

Evan's syndrome is a rare illness, and informaiton on it is hard to find. This document serves to collate relevant information I found on this topic. If you or a family member are suffering from this illness, I hope you find this helpful. I have specifically excluded any links to alternative treatments, or pages that have an angle to sell a specific product.

If you are an expert on this subject, I encourage you to please send me link to relevant documents, so I can add it, or correct any inaccuracies on this page.
